About Training & Development

Our seminars and trainings are developed by industry experts to help organizations and individuals improve their competence and expand their professional skills – the main components of sustainable business development and competitiveness.

Standards Trainings

Management Systems Standards Trainings QCS LLC trainings in management systems and management standards will give you a thorough understanding of how management systems and related standards can be applied within your organization. Our scope of trainings includes:

  • ISO 9001:2015 Awareness
  • ISO 9001:2015 Internal auditor
  • ISO 9001:2015 Lead auditor
  • ISO 14001:2015 Awareness
  • ISO 14001:2015 Internal auditor
  • ISO 14001:2015 Lead auditor
  • ISO 45001:2018 Awareness
  • ISO 45001:2018 Internal auditor
  • ISO 45001:2018 Lead auditor
  • Basic Fire Safety
  • Fire Warden
  • Basic First Aid
  • Project management Fundamentals
  • Integrated Management System (IMS) implementation
