About Our Company

Company Overview

Established in the year 2016, QCS LLC is an officially Accredited Certification Body providing certification services against ISO standards, including ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 22000, ISO 39001 etc.



We work with only the most reputable registrars to ensure your certification has the proper national and international recognition for your industry. We also ensure your registrar “partner” is well suited to work with you to continually improve your management systems and organizational effectiveness. As a Certification Body we offer the following:

  • National & International recognition of your management systems
  • International recognition of our Certificates
  • Competitive and impartial certification services for businesses of all sizes
  • Experience of most industries and business sectors
  • Low cost scheme for small businesses

We also offer training services for implementation of various management systems at the workplaces and operation sites. QCS LLC has the hands-on experience and expertise to effectively coach your employees and correctly interpret the ISO standards for your particular application. We have worked hand-in-hand with a wide variety of clients including companies in oil & gas, scaffolding, education, transportation and other fields. The organizations have ranged in size from very small to very large, with one site or multiple locations.


QCS provides software solutions – Quality Assurance, OH&S and Enviromental Management systems related web applications & other products for individual company processes.


Based in Baku, Azerbaijan the QCS LLC is striving to be a leader in the coaching and certification of companies which aimed to build their management systems in accordance with international standards. Supported by a team of efficient and expert professionals, we offer effective services to meet the varied requirements of our clients.

Our Commitment

QCS LLC is committed to complying with all applicable laws, standards, codes, client’s needs, expectations and relevant requirements to achieve high standards of quality performance. Our processes are structured into a documented Quality and Information Security Management System, which meets the requirements for ISO 9001:2015, ISO/IEC 17021:2014 and ISO 27001:2013 Standards.

1. Who can ask for your ISO certificates?

Obtaining of ISO Certificate(s) is one of the best ways to improve credibility and image of your company – because it is an internationally recognized standard. When some client organization is looking for a service or products supplier, it is often a requirement to have an IMS based on ISO 9001:2015, 45001:2018 & 14001:2015 in order to be considered

1. How can you obtain ISO certificate(s)?

For obtaining an ISO certificate, your organizations shall:

  • Comply with applicable legislation;
  • Follow the guidelines of the standard;
  • Fulfil its own requirements and those of its customers;
  • Identify risks and implement control measures;
  • Follow statutory and regulatory requirements;
  • Create documents;
  • Keep track of records.
  • Once you have well established Management System, which fully meets
  • Requirements of applicable ISO standard(s) you can apply to any
  • Officially accredited certification body, such QCS, for conduction
  • Audit of your management system.
