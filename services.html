Services

QCS LLC was established in the year 2016 as a consulting organization for implementation of quality, environmental, health & safety, transport and IT management system requirements.


In July 2018, QCS LLC become a certification body. We obtained AZS ISO/IEC 17021:2014 certificate from Azerbaijan Accreditation Center (AZAK) for the provision of certification audits against requirements of ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and ISO 22000:2018 standards.


Our organization conducts certification audits in accordance with AZS ISO/IEC 17021-1:2021 “Assessment of conformity. Requirements for bodies providing audit and certification of management systems”


We also provide software solutions – Quality Assurance, OH&S and Enviromental Management systems related web applications & other products for individual company processes.
